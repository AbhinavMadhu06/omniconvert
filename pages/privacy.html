Privacy Policy

At OmniConvert, we value your privacy and are committed to being transparent about the information we do (and do not) collect. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our website and tools.

1. No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data such as names, email addresses, or contact details. OmniConvert is a static, no-backend platform, meaning there are no databases or servers storing your activity.

2. Data Entered in Tools

Any information you enter into our conversion tools (numbers, units, text, etc.) is processed instantly in your browser. It is never sent to our servers or stored for future use.

3. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies for tracking, advertising, or analytics purposes. Your browsing activity remains private, and no third-party trackers are embedded on our site.

4. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites for additional resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party websites.

5. Security

While we do not store your data, we take security seriously. Our site uses HTTPS encryption to ensure that your connection is secure and your data is processed safely in your browser.

6. Children's Privacy

OmniConvert is not intended for children under the age of 13. As we do not collect any information, we do not knowingly gather personal data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to our site or legal requirements. We recommend reviewing this page periodically for any updates.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
